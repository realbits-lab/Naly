## Usage Notes

- Some feeds require authentication or premium subscriptions
- Update frequencies vary: news feeds update continuously during market hours, central bank feeds update around policy meetings
- Many Asian and European sources offer superior coverage in local languages
- Rate limiting may apply to high-frequency access
- Government and central bank feeds typically offer unrestricted access
- Commercial news services may impose usage limits or require attribution

## Implementation Tips

1. Use RSS aggregators like Feedly, Inoreader, or specialized financial platforms
2. Set up hierarchical monitoring: tier-one for real-time alerts, tier-two for analysis
3. Implement keyword filtering for relevant content
4. Monitor feed availability as URLs may change
5. Consider multilingual capabilities for non-English sources
6. Respect terms of service and rate limits
7. Verify feed authenticity from official sources
